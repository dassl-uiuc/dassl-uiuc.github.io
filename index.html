People

Aishwarya Ganesan

Aishwarya is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. She is interested in distributed systems, storage and file systems. Her research addresses the fundamental tradeoff between consistency and performance in distributed storage. Additionally, her work on distributed reliability has exposed many severe bugs in popular systems. Her work has appeared in top systems venues such as OSDI, SOSP, and FAST and has been recognized with best-paper awards at FAST 20 and FAST 18. She was selected for the MIT Rising Stars in EECS.

Ramnatthan Alagappan

Ram is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. He is a computer systems researcher. His work spans storage, file, and distributed systems. He is often interested in improving the reliability and performance of these systems. His work has been published at premier systems venues (SOSP, OSDI, FAST, EuroSys, and ATC), invited to journals (CACM and TOS), and has won three best-paper awards. He has developed tools that have had a practical impact, including exposing more than 80 severe bugs in 20 widely used systems.
